# TripSync — product vision (team)

## Vision statement
TripSync helps people explore a city on their own terms by turning a map pin and a short taste profile into a small set of places they might actually visit — each with a clear “why you” explanation, not a generic list.

## Who it is for
Travelers and locals who want personalized discovery without endless scrolling: people who care about mood, pace, and what to avoid.

## Problem
Map and review apps show too much undifferentiated choice. TripSync reduces overload by curating a few high-signal options near the pin, grounded in interests and day preferences.

## How might we
How might we help someone pick their next stop in under a minute using one map gesture plus minimal setup, while still feeling personal?
