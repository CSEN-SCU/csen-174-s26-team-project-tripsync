Product Vision Statement
FOR travelers exploring new locations who want to discover meaningful places without constant planning
WHO struggle to find interesting, context-rich points of interest in real time and often miss hidden gems
THE Orbit is an AI-powered interactive travel guide
THAT surfaces nearby points of interest and delivers real-time, audio-based tour guide experience as users explore
UNLIKE static map apps or pre-planned guides like Google Maps or traditional audio tours
OUR PRODUCT adapts to the user’s location and interests to proactively surface relevant places and provide conversational, context-aware insights
POWERED BY real-time location-aware recommendation and natural language generation with conversational voice interaction
